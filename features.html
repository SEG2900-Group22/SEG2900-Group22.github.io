Viewing Party

The main goal of this website is to give a platform to people that want to watch with friends and family together virtually. There are always circumstances that could cause people to not be able to meet up with friends to watch a match together or to go to actual live games. Solo has you covered for that. We have created a platform that allows people from anywhere be able to view with anyone and any popular sport with just the click of a button.

Fantasy League

There is no better way to watch the sports you love with friends without a little competition! Battle with your friends with a friendly wager and see who wins the league and becomes crowned the fantasy champion! We have created fantasy leagues for many popular sports which people know and love with many more sports to be added soon!

Blog Posts

What better is there to know if a player is injured or if a match is postponed than on the Solo website. We have live updates on players health, transfer rumours, score updates and more!
